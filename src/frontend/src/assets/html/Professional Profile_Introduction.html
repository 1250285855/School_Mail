计算机科学与技术专业简介

Introduction to Computer Science and Technology

专业导读:

计算机科学与技术是为了适应国家数字经济发展的战略需要，培养掌握计算机软硬件系统设计、人工智能和大数据分析以及产业数字化规划与建设能力的高素质复合型人才的专业。本专业将教会同学们信息与计算的理论基础以及其在计算机系统中的实现与应用。

通俗地说，我们要学习信息如何在计算机中进行表示和处理，如何通过算法和程序解决我们日常生活中的各种问题，还将学习这些方法在本学科前沿领域如人工智能、嵌入式系统、信息安全、云计算等的应用案例。我们的课程将带领同学们一步步走进丰富多彩的计算机世界，让你从“计算机小白”成长为一名有着扎实理论基础和一定实践经验的计算机工程师。

Computer science and technology is a major that aims to adapt to the strategic needs of the development of the national digital economy and cultivate high-quality composite talents who master computer software and hardware system design, artificial intelligence, big data analysis, and industrial digital planning and construction capabilities. This major will teach students the theoretical basis of information and computing, as well as its implementation and application in computer systems. Generally speaking, we will learn how to express and process information in computers, how to solve various problems in our daily life through algorithms and programs, and also learn the application cases of these methods in the frontier fields of our discipline, such as artificial intelligence, embedded systems, information security, cloud computing, etc. Our course will lead the students step by step into the colorful computer world, and let you grow from "Computer Xiaobai" to a computer engineer with a solid theoretical basis and certain practical experience.

专业优势:

国际化培养方案:核心课程采用塞浦路斯欧洲大学的国际化培养方案,尤其注重加强学生在人工智能、嵌入式系统、信息安全与云计算等前沿学科领域的知识基础。课程具备极强的实战性,编程基础课采用手到学习(hands-on learning)教学法,高年级学生通过完成顶点项目(capstone project),累积用所学内容解决实际工程问题的能力。完成学业和考核要求后，可同时获得闽江学院计算机科学与技术专业和塞浦路斯欧洲大学计算机工程专业的学位证书。

国际化师资队伍:专业教学团队汇聚闽江学院与塞浦路斯欧洲大学在计算机科学领域的最优质师资，核心教学团队教师博士毕业于美国、英国、日本、澳大利亚等国际知名学府。中方专业教师团队主要来自“计算机科学与技术”省级一流本科专业建设点，科研工作依托福建省信息处理与智能控制重点实验室、数字福建智能化生产物联网实验室等省级科研平台开展，为本科生接触各类国家级、省级和企业横向科研项目，参与专业类和科创类竞赛提供技术支撑。

优越的实验室条件:国际数字经济学院的计算机专业实验课依托“闽江学院省级计算机实验教学示范中心”开展。实验室设备齐全，条件优越，为培养学生的实践能力提供了保障。学院还建有面向本科生开放的“学生科创中心”，为学生在课后参与各类学科竞赛和教师科研项目提供了场地和设备保障。

Internationalized training program: The core curriculum adopts the international training program of the European University of Cyprus, with special emphasis on strengthening students' knowledge base in cutting-edge disciplines such as artificial intelligence, embedded systems, information security and cloud computing. The course is highly practical. The basic programming course adopts the hand on learning teaching method. Senior students can accumulate the ability to solve practical engineering problems by completing the capstone project. After completing the study and examination requirements, you can obtain the degree certificate of computer science and technology from Minjiang University and computer engineering from European University of Cyprus.Internationalized teaching team: The professional teaching team gathers the best quality teachers in the field of computer science from Minjiang University and European University of Cyprus. The core teaching team graduated from the United States, Britain, Japan, Australia and other internationally renowned universities with doctorates. The Chinese professional teacher team mainly comes from the provincial first-class undergraduate professional construction site of "Computer Science and Technology". The scientific research work is carried out by relying on the provincial scientific research platforms such as Fujian Key Laboratory of Information Processing and Intelligent Control, Digital Fujian Intelligent Production Internet of Things Laboratory, to provide technical support for undergraduates to contact various national, provincial and enterprise horizontal scientific research projects and participate in professional and scientific innovation competitions.Superior laboratory conditions: The computer professional experimental courses of the International Digital Economics College are carried out by relying on the "Provincial Computer Experimental Teaching Demonstration Center of Minjiang University". The laboratory is well equipped and has superior conditions, providing guarantee for training students' practical ability. The college also has a "student science and innovation center" open to undergraduates, which provides venues and equipment for students to participate in various discipline competitions and teacher research projects after class.

专业类竞赛:

软件与程序设计类: ACM-ICPC国际大学生程序设计竞赛、中国大学生计算机设计大赛、海峡两岸信息服务创新大赛暨计算机软件设计大赛、中国大学生程序设计大赛、泛珠三角计算机作品赛等。

硬件与系统集成类:中国智能制造挑战赛、中国大学生智能设计大赛、全国大学生物联网设计竞赛、单片机应用设计赛、大学生智能设计大赛、SDN应用创新大赛等。

网络与信息安全类:全国大学生信息安全大赛、网络安全防护赛暨工业互联网安全大赛、信息安全铁人三项赛、工业控制系统信息安全攻防大赛等。

自动控制类:全国大学生机器人大赛RoboMaster、RobotChallenge、全国大学生智能汽车竞赛等。

创新创业类:“挑战杯”全国大学生课外学术科技作品竞赛、中国国际“互联网+”大学生创新创业大赛、“创青春”中国青年创新创业大赛等。

Software and programming: ACM-ICPC International Undergraduate Programming Competition, China Undergraduate Computer Design Competition, Cross Strait Information Service Innovation Competition and Computer Software Design Competition, China Undergraduate Programming Competition, Pan Pearl River Delta Computer Works Competition, etc. Hardware and system integration: China Intelligent Manufacturing Challenge, China Undergraduate Intelligent Design Competition, National University Bionetworking Design Competition, SCM Application Design Competition, Undergraduate Intelligent Design Competition, SDN Application Innovation Competition, etc. Network and information security: National Undergraduate Information Security Competition, Network Security Protection Competition and Industrial Internet Security Competition, Information Security Triathlon, Industrial Control System Information Security Attack and Defense Competition, etc. Automatic control: RoboMaster, RobotChallenge, National Undergraduate Robot Competition, etc. Innovation and entrepreneurship: "Challenge Cup" National Undergraduate Extracurricular Academic Science and Technology Works Competition, China International "Internet plus" Undergraduate Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, "Youth Creation" China Youth Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition, etc.

计算机专业学生发展前景

本专业培养的学生具有很好的发展前景。学生毕业后可以到国内外众多软件企业、互联网公司、大型电信服务商、大型通信设备制造企业、国家机关以及各个大中型企、事业单位的信息技术部门、教育部门等单位从事技术开发、教学、科研及管理等工作,也可以继续攻读计算机科学与技术类专业研究生。细分岗位很多,包括计算机语言工程师(如Java工程师)、计算机软件测试、计算机图形图像制作、计算机办公应用、计算机数据库管理、计算机可视化编程、计算机WEB应用程序设计、网络技术、多媒体制作、网络系统管理等。

The students trained in this major have good prospects for development. After graduation, students can engage in technology development, teaching, scientific research and management in many domestic and foreign software enterprises, Internet companies, large telecommunications service providers, large communication equipment manufacturing enterprises, state organs, information technology departments of various large and medium-sized enterprises and institutions, education departments and other units, or continue to study computer science and technology graduate students. There are many subdivided posts, including computer language engineers (such as Java engineers), computer software testing, computer graphics and images production, computer office applications, computer database management, computer visual programming, computer WEB application program design, network technology, multimedia production, network system management, etc.
